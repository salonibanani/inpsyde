Why galvanizing

Our corporate values have been instilled across various facets of our business and impel us to succeed further.

Today, the Group’s business activities encompass a spectrum of industries and a portfolio that includes several internationally acclaimed companies.

In a few decades, Qatar has chronicled a meteoric rise by achieving remarkable development in various sectors that spurred its economy and elevated the country’s status on a global level. Capitalizing on its rich oil and gas reserves and steered by the far-sighted vision of its rulers, Qatar accomplished momentous milestones that galvanized its economy.

Where We Stand

Today, Qatar takes pride in this astonishing progress and lays claim to two significant achievements. It is the first country to export natural gas internationally in addition to boasting the world’s highest GDP per capita income. In parallel to Qatar’s feats, Almana Group harnessed the abundant opportunities provided by the country in its quest for progress.

Qatar's Pride

More About Almana Group.....

Originating as a trading house, the Group today is an enterprise that comprises a wide array of services and business activities.
