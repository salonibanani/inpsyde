A LUXURY HOTEL

Cielo Hotel believes in creating a memorable experience for its customers. Our Arabic manner combined with modern comfort welcoming inspire us to transform the traditional into luxury experiences. Our services elevate the level of experience & satisfaction.

The entire hotel is air-conditioned and facilitated with various services like a fitness area, swimming pool, well-furnished rooms, Wi-Fi connectivity, and much more to experience.

Cielo Hotel has 70 rooms, four floors having a swimming pool on a rooftop in the superior, deluxe, and suite category. The design and decor of all the rooms are native to the Arabian Night Tale. The rooms are fully furnished with all necessary amenities like latest design, comfortable beds & sofas, smart TVs, charging stands, office table and many more. 

The stunning interior and chic look make it an ideal hotel for those who want to experience comprehensive hospitality services.
